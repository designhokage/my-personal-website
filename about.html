About me

Hello! I'm Emmanuel Edigbe
and I'm a User Experience Designer.

As a kid I wanted to become a Psychologist, then a Medical Doctor, to studying Botany at the University. One thing led to another and I'm currently studying New Media Design at Jonkoping University in Sweden.

I believe that design must support functionality, and that design is more than just choosing colours, typefaces, shapes and images. Good design works to present information in such a way as to support the information
it presents.

I enjoy listening to music, watching sports, playing fantasy premier league and playing FIFA.

Feel free to look at my portfolio and don't hesitate to contact me if you think I can be of service to you. Thanks for stopping by.
